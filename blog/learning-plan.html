Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

To work as a full-stack developer.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning:

Strengths: When others are reliant upon me learning something, then I will ensure that I understand/complete the learning content.

Limitations: Without deadlines or others’ expectations I often don’t engage with learning content in a timely manner.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I’d like to develop the ability to reach out to others.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way:

If I cannot work something out myself within 30 minutes then I will seek help from my peers. If my peers cannot help me within a few hours then I will get help from a Dev Academy facilitator.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team:

To respond to reasonable student enquiries to the best of their abilities during standard working hours.

Any scheduling information:

I have no scheduled commitments to others that would impact my learning at Dev Academy.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme:

To work productively I’ll set goals at the start of every day and manage my time appropriately.

To work productively and safely with others involved in the Dev Academy programme I’ll aim to implement actions and behaviours that align with the core values of integrity, kindness and effort.

When the workload builds up and starts getting to me I’ll take a step back and look at the bigger picture. When looking at the bigger picture I can often see that a few things have been taking up my attention for too long, and that I should move on to the next thing to continue progressing.
